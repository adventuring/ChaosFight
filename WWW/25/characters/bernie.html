Bernie

Bernie can't jump, but he can fall off the bottom of the screen and reappear at the top! His unique "Ground Thump" attack hits both in front AND behind simultaneously, making him dangerous in crowds. He thumps the ground so hard that it affects anyone to either side of him, shoving them rapidly away! Bernie's tough constitution grants complete immunity to fall damage.

About Bernie

Original Game: Bernie and the Towers of Doom (Atari 7800)

Author: Lewis "Muddyfunster" Hill of Muddyvision

Weight: 5 kg (10 lbs) | Attack: Ground Thump (Melee, BOTH DIRECTIONS!)

Special: No jumping; wraps from bottom to top of screen; fall damage immunity
