Dragon of Storms

The Dragon of Storms is one of two legendary dragons who united their strengths to confront the Seven Dragon Monarchs. As the master of storms, this dragon can hover freely (no gravity) and fires fireballs, making them excellent for hitting enemies. The Dragon of Storms brings the power of wind and tempest to the arena, a formidable force that has faced down monarchs and armies alike.

About Dragon of Storms

Original Game: Dragon's Descent, Dragon's Cache, and Dragon's Havoc (Dragon's Trilogy)

Author: Todd "Revontulli" Furmanski

Weight: 1590 kg (3500 lbs) | Attack: Ranged

Special: Can fly (no gravity)
