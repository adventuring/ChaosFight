Frooty

Captain of Queen Sugar's royal guard, Frooty is loyal, powerful, hyper, and ever so slightly insane. Frooty can fly freely up and down using the joystick! However, she cannot guard (pressing DOWN makes her fly down instead). Her ranged attack fires magical sparkles from her lollipop weapon, combining whimsical charm with deadly combat prowess.

About Frooty

Original Game: Magical Fairy Force, Championship, and Drop

Author: Ryan Witmer - Phaser Cat Games

Weight: 55 kg (120 lbs) | Attack: Ranged (magical sparkles)

Special: Can fly using UP/DOWN to move vertically (no guard action)

Likes: Candy | Hates: Boredom
