Harpy

Swift of wing and vicious in nature, the Harpy was once exiled to a perilous labyrinth by the Great Sphinx. Now free of the maze, she brings that hard-won combat experience to ChaosFight. The Harpy can maintain flight by repeatedly flapping her wings, and her attack causes her to swoop down diagonally while performing a devastating strike. These techniques were perfected during her trials against the four guardians and the Great Sphinx.

About Harpy

Original Game: Harpy's Curse

Author: Todd "Revontulli" Furmanski

Weight: 15 kg (30 lbs) | Attack: Melee (diagonal swoop)

Special: Can fly by repeatedly pressing UP (flapping wings)
