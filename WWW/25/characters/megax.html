Megax

Megax is a Grizzard - a giant monster-eating powerhouse, the biggest and most powerful Grizzard known. He breathes fire, unleashing devastating flames that strike adjacent enemies. As the mightiest of all Grizzards, Megax brings incredible strength and size to the arena, making him a formidable opponent with his unique fire breath attacks and massive presence.

About Megax

Original Game: Grizzards

Author: Zephyr & Bruce-Robert Pocock (Interworldly Adventuring, LLC)

Species: Grizzard (giant monster-eating powerhouse)

Weight: 1590 kg (3500 lbs) | Attack: Melee (fire breath)

Special: Breathing fire is a normal ability
