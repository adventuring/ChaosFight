Nefertem

Nefertem is a mysterious cat, as big as a man, with gray and black stripes, white paws, and a white chest. He hails from the ancient realm of Aclypt, where he serves as a steadfast loyalist to the High Council. As a heavy mêlée fighter, Nefertem relies on powerful close-combat attacks and his substantial weight to overwhelm opponents. His feline reflexes and council training make him a dangerous adversary in any arena.

About Nefertem

Original Game: Phantasia

Author: Zephyr & Bruce-Robert Pocock (Interworldly Adventuring, LLC)
