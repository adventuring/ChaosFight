Pork Chop

Pork Chop has had a diverse career across multiple games - from working with magical fairies to bank security, from sewer maintenance to the precise sport of curling. His heavy build and reliable melee attacks reflect the steady, dependable nature that has served him well across all his various careers. His diverse work experience has given him a well-rounded skill set and the determination to handle any situation thrown at him.

About Pork Chop

Original Game: Ratcatcher, Magical Fairy Force, and Rob 'N' Banks

Author: Ryan Witmer - Phaser Cat Games

Weight: 115 kg (250 lbs) | Attack: Melee

Career History: Fairy employee, bank guard, sewer cleaner, curling athlete
