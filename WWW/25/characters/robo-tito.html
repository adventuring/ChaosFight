Robo Tito

Robo Tito (a.k.a "Tito el robotito") is a jumpless platformer character, a robot that can't jump, but he can stretch to reach platforms and hang from them. Due to a programming error in his system, he is allergic to scary things. In ChaosFight, his stretch ability works the same way - press UP to stretch to the ceiling and latch on, then press DOWN to return to the floor. His robotic construction ensures he takes only limited fall damage compared to other characters.

About Robo Tito

Original Game: Tito el robotito contra los fantasmas (jumpless platformer)

Weight: 135 kg (300 lbs) | Attack: Mêlée

Special: Can stretch vertically to ceiling (press UP to latch, DOWN to release); takes limited fall damage

Programming Error: Allergic to scary things (zombies, ghosts, bats, skulls)
