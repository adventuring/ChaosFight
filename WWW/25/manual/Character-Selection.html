4 Character Selection ¶

After the Title screen, each player chooses their fighter by cycling through the available characters.

  • The Character Selection Screen
  • Selection Controls
  • Starting the Game
  • Handicapping for Advanced Players

4.1 The Character Selection Screen ¶

The screen shows characters in fixed positions.

4.2 Selection Controls ¶

LEFT/RIGHT

Cycle through characters

FIRE

Lock in character selection

  • Playfield border appears around locked character
  • Moving joystick after locking will unlock selection
  • Hold DOWN when pressing FIRE to handicap your character.

A "handicap" for your character starts you with less health. This is to allow for a more fair fight if playing against a novice player.

4.3 Starting the Game ¶

The following game play configurations are supported:

  • 1 player To play alone, leave Player 2 set to CPU and select a character as Player 1. When you press FIRE, the computer will choose a random character to face off with you, and the game will start immediately.

    If a Quadtari is connected, players 3 and 4 must both be left on "NO."

  • 2 player To play with two players, move Player 2’s selection off "CPU." Both players must lock in their character with FIRE to proceed.

    If a Quadtari is connected, players 3 and 4 must both be left on "NO."

    You cannot start a 3- or 4- player game with a CPU player.

  • 3 player To play with three players, a character must be selected for Players 1 and 2, and either Player 3 or 4 must move off "NO" and select a character. No CPU player is allowed.
  • 4 player To play with four players, all four players must select a character and then lock them in with FIRE.

If Player 1 locks in their character selection before the other players, it could start the game. It is advised to let all players make their selections before Player 1 presses Fire.

4.4 Handicapping for Advanced Players ¶

Skilled players can start with reduced health for added challenge.

Activating Handicap Mode:

  1. Select your character with LEFT/RIGHT
  2. Hold DOWN on the joystick
  3. While holding DOWN, press FIRE to lock selection

A character will appear "kneeling" (or similar) to indicate the handicapping is enabled.

Starting health: 75% instead of 100%
