6.1.1 Bernie ¶

    Weight: 5 kg (10 lbs)
    Attack: Ground Thump (Mêlée, BOTH DIRECTIONS!)
    Special: No jumping; wraps from bottom to top of screen; fall damage immunity
    Original Game: Bernie and the Towers of Doom (Atari 7800)
    Author: Lewis "Muddyfunster" Hill of Muddyvision Muddyvision
    Purchase: Bernie and the Tower of Doom at AtariAge Store; Bernie and the Tower of Doom on Atari VCS eShop
    Download: Bernie and the Tower of Doom free download
    Forum: Bernie and the Tower of Doom release thread

Bernie cannot jump, but he can fall off the bottom of the screen and reappear at the top!

His unique "Ground Thump" attack hits both in front AND behind simultaneously, making him dangerous in crowds. He thumps the ground so hard that it affects anyone to either side of him, shoving them rapidly away!

Bernie’s tough constitution grants complete immunity to fall damage.

6.1.3 DragonOfStorms ¶

    Weight: 1590 kg (3500 lbs)
    Attack: Ranged
    Special: Can fly (no gravity)
    Original Game: Dragon’s Descent, Dragon’s Cache, and Dragon’s Havoc (Dragon’s Trilogy)
    Author: Todd "Revontulli" Furmanski
    Character: Dragon of Storms (alongside Dragon of Embers in Dragon’s Havoc)
    Purchase: Dragon’s Descent at AtariAge Store; Dragon’s Cache at AtariAge Store; Dragon’s Havoc at AtariAge Store; Dragon’s Havoc on Atari VCS eShop
    Forum: Dragon’s Cache & Descent release thread; Dragon’s Havoc release thread

The Dragon of Storms is one of two legendary dragons who united their strengths to confront the Seven Dragon Monarchs. These monarchs have unleashed armies of griffins, harpies, and other beasts upon the land, threatening all who stand in their way. Alongside the Dragon of Embers, the Dragon of Storms must battle through seven stages, each containing outdoor areas, indoor sections, and confrontations with the powerful Dragon Monarchs themselves.

As the master of storms, this dragon can hover freely (no gravity) and fires fireballs, making them excellent for hitting enemies. The Dragon of Storms brings the power of wind and tempest to the arena, a formidable force that has faced down monarchs and armies alike.

6.1.7 Harpy ¶

    Weight: 15 kg (30 lbs)
    Attack: Mêlée (diagonal swoop)
    Special: Can fly by repeatedly pressing UP (flapping wings)
    Background: Former prisoner of the deadly labyrinth, defeated the Great Sphinx
    Nature: Swift of wing and vicious in combat
    Experience: Veteran of maze battles, collector of legendary artifacts
    Original Game: Harpy’s Curse
    Author: Todd "Revontulli" Furmanski
    Purchase: Harpy’s Curse at AtariAge Store; Harpy’s Curse on Atari VCS eShop
    Forum: Harpy’s Curse release thread

You are a harpy - swift of wing and vicious in nature. Once exiled to a perilous labyrinth by the Great Sphinx, you were forced to navigate through corridors of rock, brick, and bone. Your mission was to locate and defeat four guardians before confronting the Great Sphinx herself to secure your freedom. Throughout your journey, you uncovered hidden secrets, evaded deadly traps, and battled various denizens of the maze. The labyrinth contained legendary artifacts - feathers of legendary birds and beasts that aided your escape and enhanced your powers.

Now free of the maze, you bring that hard-won combat experience to ChaosFight. Your time battling through the labyrinth’s dangers has honed your aerial combat skills to perfection. The Harpy can maintain flight by repeatedly flapping her wings (jump button or up on the joystick), and her attack causes her to swoop down diagonally while performing a devastating strike. These techniques were perfected during your trials against the four guardians and the Great Sphinx.

6.1.9 Frooty ¶

    Age: 24
    Weight: 55 kg (120 lbs)
    Attack: Ranged (magical sparkles)
    Special: Can fly using UP/DOWN to move vertically (no guard action)
    Likes: Candy
    Hates: Boredom
    Original Game: Magical Fairy Force, Championship, and Drop
    Author: Ryan Witmer Phaser Cat Games
    Purchase: Magical Fairy Force at AtariAge Store; "Magical Fairy Force Championship Adventure on Steam"; Fairy Drop at AtariAge Store
    Forum: Magical Fairy Force release thread; Fairy Drop release thread

Captain of Queen Sugar’s royal guard, Frooty is loyal, powerful, hyper, and ever so slightly insane. As the one charged with the defence of Fairy Land, she carries an enormous responsibility as she leads the queen’s armies into battle in Magical Fairy Force. Her role expanded in the Championship and Drop games, where she continued to serve as a key protector of the magical realm.

Frooty also oversees a number of secret research projects in Fairy Land, some of which are finally starting to produce results. Her dedication to both military strategy and magical research makes her a formidable leader, even if her hyperactive personality and love of candy sometimes mask her serious responsibilities.

Frooty can fly freely up and down using the joystick! However, she cannot guard (pressing DOWN makes her fly down instead). Her ranged attack fires magical sparkles from her lollipop weapon. This generic fairy archetype represents the classic fairy fighter perfectly, combining whimsical charm with deadly combat prowess.

    Weight: 115 kg (250 lbs)
    Attack: Mêlée
    Special: (none)
    Career History: Fairy employee, bank guard, sewer cleaner, curling athlete
    Former Team: Team Koe (Edmonton) member(14)
    Original Game: Ratcatcher, Magical Fairy Force, and Rob ’N’ Banks
    Author: Ryan Witmer Phaser Cat Games
    Purchase: Rob ’N’ Banks at AtariAge Store; Magical Fairy Force at AtariAge Store; Ratcatcher at AtariAge Store; Magical Fairy Force Championship Adventure on Steam
    Forum: Rob ’N’ Banks release thread; Magical Fairy Force release thread; Ratcatcher release thread; Fairy Drop release thread

Pork Chop has had a diverse career across multiple games. In Magical Fairy Force, he worked as an employee for the fairies of Fairy Land, serving alongside Queen Sugar’s royal guard and learning the ways of magical combat. His time in the sewers as a ratcatcher gave him experience in close-quarters combat against dangerous infestations, honing his skills in Ratcatcher. As a bank guard in Rob ’N’ Banks, he protected financial institutions from robbers, developing defensive tactics and situational awareness. His brief stint on the Edmonton curling team (Team Koe) taught him precision and teamwork.

From the delicate work with magical fairies to the rough-and-tumble world of bank security, from the unpleasant but necessary sewer maintenance to the precise sport of curling, Pork Chop has learned to adapt to any environment. His heavy build and reliable mêlée attacks reflect the steady, dependable nature that has served him well across all his various careers. His diverse work experience has given him a well-rounded skill set and the determination to handle any situation thrown at him.

6.1.14 Robo Tito ¶

    Weight: 135 kg (300 lbs)
    Attack: Mêlée
    Special: Can stretch vertically to ceiling (press UP to latch, DOWN to release); takes limited fall damage
    Original Game: Tito el robotito contra los fantasmas (jumpless platformer)
    Programming Error: Allergic to scary things (zombies, ghosts, bats, skulls)
    Purchase: Robo Tito at AtariAge Store
    Forum: Robo Tito release thread

Robo Tito (a.k.a "Tito el robotito") is a jumpless platformer character, a robot that can’t jump, but he can stretch to reach platforms and hang from them.

Due to a programming error in his system, he is allergic to scary things. To his misfortune he is trapped in a place infested with scary things (zombies, ghosts, bats, skulls), so he has to look for an exit, avoiding touching those spooky items.

Use your joystick controller to move Tito left and right along the floor. You can press UP on your controller to stretch Tito up to the ceiling, which he will then grab and hang on to. You can then move Tito left and right along the ceiling. To get back down to the floor, press DOWN on your joystick until Tito reaches the floor.

In ChaosFight, his stretch ability works the same way - press UP to stretch to the ceiling and latch on, then press DOWN to return to the floor. His robotic construction ensures he takes only limited fall damage compared to other characters.

6.1.16 Shamone ¶

    Weight: 30 kg (65 lbs)
    Attack: Mêlée
    Special: Press UP to transform
    Mission: Protect vegetable gardens from nasty ducks
    Motivation: Dedicated guardian against persistent duck attacks
    Original Game: Ducks Away
    Author: SmittyB
    Purchase: Ducks Away at AtariAge Store
    Forum: Ducks Away release thread

Shamone just wants to protect your vegetable garden from these nasty ducks, but they won’t stop attacking. This loyal guardian has developed fierce fighting skills through constant battles to defend precious vegetables from relentless waterfowl invaders.

Despite being a light mêlée fighter, Shamone’s dedication to garden protection has made them surprisingly tenacious in combat.

Shamone has a unique transformation ability - press UP to transform into a different form that shares all of Shamone’s combat abilities. This ability to switch forms gives Shamone unexpected versatility in battle.
