10.3 Tools Used ¶

ChaosFight was created with:

  • batariBasic - High-level BASIC compiler for Atari 2600
  • DASM - Macro assembler
  • SkylineTool - Asset conversion toolkit
  • GIMP - Graphics editing
  • MuseScore - Music and sound composition
  • Emacs - Integrated development environment
  • Stella - Atari 2600 emulator for testing
  • Cursor - AI assisted development
