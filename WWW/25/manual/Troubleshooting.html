9.1 Common Issues ¶

  • Controllers Not Responding
  • Four Players Not Working
  • Game Won’t Start from Multicart

9.1.1 Controllers Not Responding ¶

Problem: Additional controller buttons (C, II, III) are not detected.

Solutions:

  1. Check that controllers are firmly connected to the correct ports
  2. Try pressing Game Select at the Title screen to trigger detection
  3. Extra buttons are not supported through the Quadtari.

9.1.2 Four Players Not Working ¶

Problem: Only 2 players are active despite Quadtari being connected.

Solutions:

  1. Press GAME SELECT on the Title or Character Select screen to re-detect the Quadtari

9.1.3 Game Won’t Start from Multicart ¶

Problem: ChaosFight doesn’t launch from multicart menu.

Solutions:

  1. Ensure you have the correct ROM version for your multicart
  2. Some multicarts may require the ".a26" extension to be renamed to ".EFSC" for proper detection.
  3. When launching from a multicart on Atari 7800, your console may not be detected as a 7800, and "Black & White" mode will be only enabled while you hold down the "Pause" button. There is no workaround for this at present.

9.2 Getting Help ¶

If you continue to experience issues:

  1. Check for ROM updates at https://interworldly.com/ or the AtariAge Homebrew forums.
  2. Watch ZeroPage Homebrew on Twitch and YouTube for news and updates about Atari VCS homebrew games including Chaos Fight.
  3. Consult AtariAge community forums for technical support (Homebrew Chat forum)
  4. Contact Interworldly Adventuring at support@interworldly.com
  5. Contact Bruce-Robert Pocock in the AtariAge Discord (Homebrew Chat forum)
