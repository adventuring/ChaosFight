Curling Sweeper

The Curling Sweeper fires a curling stone that appears without elaboration and slides across the floor. Great for hitting low targets! A proud Cascadian and Canadian, very sportif.

About Curling Sweeper

Original Game: RealSports Curling (Atari 5200)

Author: Ryan Witmer - Phaser Cat Games

Weight: 85 kg (190 lbs) | Attack: Ranged (from feet, ground-based)

Special: Curling stone appears without elaboration
