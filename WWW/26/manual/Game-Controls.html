5 Game Controls ¶

  • Basic Movement
  • Guarding
  • Health and Damage
  • Victory

5.1 Basic Movement ¶

  • LEFT/RIGHT Move your character left or right and turn to face that direction
  • UP Jump (or character-specific special move)
  • DOWN Guard (or character-specific special move)
  • FIRE/B/I button Attack

5.2 Guarding ¶

Enter guard stance by holding DOWN. While guarding:

  • You take NO damage from attacks
  • You cannot move or attack
  • Guard lasts maximum 1 second
  • Guard cannot be used again for 1 second after previous use
  • You can still be picked up, thrown, and knocked back while guarding
  • Character turns cyan while the guard is active

5.3 Health and Damage ¶

Player 1 and 2’s health are displayed as colored bars at the bottom of the screen.

If player 3 or 4 are present, their health will display as a percentage (00-99) in the middle of the bottom of the screen.

When your health reaches 0, you are eliminated and your character disappears from the playfield.

Character weight affects knockback - heavier characters resist being pushed back more than lighter characters. When hit, lighter characters will be knocked back further than heavier ones.

5.4 Victory ¶

The last player with health remaining wins!

In a 3- or 4- player game, the winner will appear at the top of the screen, with other characters arranged below in elimination order (second, third, and last place, where last place is first eliminated).
