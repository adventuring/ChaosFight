Welcome to the Arena

Step into the ultimate crossover fighting experience! Chaos Fight brings together 16 beloved characters from your favorite AtariAge homebrew games for epic battles on the Atari 2600.

Whether you're a fan of Bernie's ground-pounding antics, Frooty's magical sparkles, or Megax's fire-breathing fury, there's a fighter here for everyone. Each character brings their unique abilities, weight classes, and combat styles straight from their original games.

Supports 1-4 players with standard controllers or the Quadtari adapter. Choose your fighter, pick your arena, and may the best character win!

Features

  • • 16 Unique Fighters - From Bernie to Shamone, each with distinct abilities and playstyles
  • • 4-Player Support - Battle with up to 4 players using Quadtari adapter
  • • 16 Arenas - Fight across diverse battlefields
  • • Weight-Based Physics - Heavy characters resist knockback, light characters jump higher
  • • Multiple Attack Types - Mêlée, ranged, and special abilities unique to each fighter
  • • NTSC, PAL, and SECAM - Available for all TV standards
